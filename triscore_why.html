Understanding the Tri-Score System

Empowering Insurance Agents with Multi-Dimensional Risk Assessment

Comprehensive Risk Assessment

The Risk Score.provides a nuanced view of potential risks, enabling agents to pinpoint specific risk factors associated with fraud, abuse, or errors in provider activities.

Scope of Practice Verification

The Out-of-Scope Probability score is essential for verifying that a provider's services are within their professional scope, ensuring the legitimacy of their practice and safeguarding against misrepresentation.

Normative Behavior Analysis

The Outlier Score identifies significant deviations from standard practice patterns, highlighting outliers that often indicate problematic behavior that requires further investigation.

Tri-Score Benefits:

  • Enhanced decision-making accuracy
  • Multi-dimensional risk analysis
  • Improved detection of fraud and abuse
  • Greater confidence in case prioritization
  • Dynamic and robust agent workflow

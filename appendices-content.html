Appendix A: Glossary of Terms

  • Anomaly Detection: Identification of items or events that do not conform to an expected pattern.
  • Claims Data: Information used by healthcare providers for billing insurers.
  • Dimensionality Reduction: Process to reduce the number of variables under consideration.
  • F1 Score: A measure of a model's accuracy, used for binary classification.
  • HIPAA: Legislation for data privacy and security of medical information.
  • Outlier: A data point significantly different from others.
  • Precision-Recall Curve (AUPRC): A graph showing the trade-off between precision and recall.
  • Risk Score: A numerical likelihood of an event, like fraud or error.
